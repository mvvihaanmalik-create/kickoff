A perfectly normal web page

This is just a plain page standing in for whatever you'd actually be browsing. The Mental Cache overlay is floating on top of it.

Try it: press Ctrl/Cmd Shift K (or click the ＋, bottom-left), type a thought, hit Enter.
A ball appears carrying your thought — kick it around. Keep boots it into the goal with a celebration; Done sends it flying until it dissolves. Click the round puck at the right edge to open everything you've kept: search it, filter by #tags, edit a thought in place, park one for Later, or open Recap for your stats and streak.

Notice you can still select this text, click this link, and scroll — the overlay never blocks the page.
