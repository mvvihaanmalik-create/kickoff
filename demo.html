Pretend this is any website.

You're browsing. A thought pops up. Toss it to the ball and get back to what you were doing.

1 Press Ctrl/Cmd Shift K, type what's on your mind, hit Enter.

2 It becomes a little football. Give it a kick. Go on.

3 Done with it? Boot it into the goal to keep it, or flick it away to let it go.

Everything you keep lives in the ball at the right edge — click it to search, tag, or revisit later. Nothing leaves your browser, ever.

Thanks for kicking it around. Built just for the fun of it — peek at how it works, and come say hi if you like little experiments like this. ⚽
